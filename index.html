Personal Bio

I am 22 years old and I am originally from Cedar Rapids, IA. I am the youngest of my family which consistes of my dad (Greg), mom (Marci), sisters (Taylor and Melanie) and myself. I grew up a huge sports fan and enjoyed going to a lot of Iowa Hawkeye sporting events. I also participated in many sports and activities growing up such as baseball, basketball, football, choir, show choir and more. I graduated from Xavier High School in 2018. After spending a year at Creighton University in Omaha, NE, I transfered to the University of Iowa.

I currently am a Senior in the Tippie College of Business at The University of Iowa. I am majoring in Business Analytics & Information Systems and Management with a minor in Sports Recreation and Management. I am currently a member of the Lambda Chi Alpha fraternity here at Iowa and have served as our Academic Chairman and Standards Chairman over the past year. In my free time I enjoy spending time with my friends and my girlfriend (Caitlin). I really enjoy watching tv, movies, and sporting events, as well as going out to dinner and cheering on the Hawkeyes. I will graduate in May of 2022. After graduation, I hope to find a career where I can utilize data in a formal leadership position.
